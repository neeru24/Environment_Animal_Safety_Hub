Glass Mystery

How long does glass last? 🥛

Mind-Blowing! 🤯

It takes 1 MILLION years for a glass bottle to decompose in nature!

Action: Always recycle glass bottles!

Tree Power

Can trees talk? 🌳

Yes They Can! 🌲

Trees use an underground "Wood Wide Web" of fungi to communicate with each other!

Action: Plant a tree and join the network!

Paper Saving

Why recycle paper? 📄

Super Saver! 📰

One ton of recycled paper saves 17 trees and 7,000 gallons of water!

Action: Use both sides of paper!

Space Junk

Is there trash in space? 🚀

Shocking Truth! 🛸

There are over 500,000 pieces of space junk orbiting Earth right now!

Action: Keep Earth clean to prevent more!

Animal Friends

Do animals have emotions? 🐾

They Feel Too! ❤️

Animals feel happiness, sadness, and fear just like humans. Be kind to all creatures!

Action: Keep water for birds & strays!

Ocean Plastic

How much plastic is in the ocean? 🌊

Crisis Alert! 🚨

By 2050, there will be more plastic than fish in the ocean if we don't act now!

Action: Say NO to single-use plastic!
